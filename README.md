# Sophia AI — Intelligent Real Estate Sales Assistant

A modern AI-powered sales assistant designed to help real estate businesses convert website visitors into potential customers through intelligent conversations.

Most property websites only display information.

Visitors explore listings, compare prices, and leave without sharing their requirements.

Sophia AI changes that experience.

Instead of a passive website, Sophia creates an interactive sales journey where customers can ask questions, discover properties, and connect with the business naturally.




## Project Vision

The goal behind Sophia AI is simple:

> Transform a traditional real estate website into a smart digital sales assistant.

This project focuses on combining:

- Artificial Intelligence
- Full Stack Development
- Sales Automation
- Customer Experience Design

to solve a real business problem.




# Core Features


## AI Sales Conversation

Sophia acts like a virtual property consultant.

It can interact with visitors, understand their requirements, and guide conversations in a more natural way compared to traditional contact forms.


Example conversations:

- Looking for a property
- Exploring investment options
- Asking about requirements
- Discussing preferences





## Intelligent Property Assistance

Sophia helps users discover suitable property options by understanding important customer preferences.

It considers information like:

- Customer intent
- Preferred property type
- Location interests
- Budget preferences
- Lifestyle requirements


The goal is to create a personalized discovery experience.





## Smart Lead Management

Every business needs quality leads.

Sophia helps capture potential customer information during conversations and organizes it for business follow-up.

This reduces missed opportunities from website visitors.





## Premium Website Experience

Designed with a modern customer experience approach:

-  Floating AI assistant  
-  Responsive design  
-  Smooth conversation flow  
-  Premium interface  
-  Mobile-friendly experience
